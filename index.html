Welcome,

Deepak Engineering Works (DEW) which was established in 1979 by Mr K K Sadanandan as a fabrication and maintenance unit at Brahmapuram, Kochi and later DEW developed into a multifaceted EPC group engaged in the field of construction of Buildings, Bridges, Roads, Piling works, Fabrication & Erection of Heavy Structural’s, Cross country Pipe lines, In plant Piping, Heavy Machinery Erection, Fire Fighting system, Design, Fabrication and Erection of Chimneys & Storage Tanks, Erection of Boiler & IBR Piping, Project Management Consultancy, Infrastructural Developments like Aviation Fuel farm, Water Supply, Ship building, Telecommunication, Penstock piping, Railways etc. DEW has diversified its business viz: DEW Tech India Pvt Ltd. Today we are one of the leading EPC Company.

For the last 40 years DEW have executed more than 300 projects for India's leading companies like BPCL, KRL, KIAL, FACT, IOCL, KWA, INFOPARK, L&T, B&R, Thermax, Cochin Shipyard, Cochin Port, MRPL, Nitta Gelatin Ltd, Indian Hume Pipe Ltd, Hindustan Newsprint Limited, Idea, JSW,SIFL, NTPC, KSEB, KINFRA, Oil Palm India Ltd, Kerala PWD, HMT, HOC, MHTC, BSNL, Indian Navy, Indian Railway, FCIL etc.
